Our Origin

Built on One
Conviction.

Advising.com was founded on a single, unfashionable belief: that in the age of artificial intelligence and algorithmically generated insight, the most valuable thing in the world is still a room full of the right people — and one advisor who has already sat in it.

Mickey Hardy and Brian Evans didn't build Advising.com because the market was ready for it. They built it because the market needed it. Between them, they have navigated the corridors of Web3, the boardrooms of venture capital, the pitch decks of breakout startups, and the quiet handshakes that close nine-figure deals. Their approach is not theoretical. It is earned.

Mickey Hardy
The Closer

The architect of Web3's most explosive brand moments — and the strategic mind behind the deals that followed them.

Mickey Hardy is the Founder and Chairman of Arcadia, the premier Web3 marketing and growth firm behind the explosive rise of category-defining protocols. Under Mickey's leadership, Arcadia engineered the go-to-market strategies for Worldcoin, Shiba Inu, Aethir, 0G Labs, and a growing roster of blue-chip digital asset projects. Arcadia's signature is precision: white-glove influencer campaigns, virality-by-design growth systems, and launch execution that generates market movement from day one.

What sets Mickey apart from conventional growth operators is his understanding that community is capital. In Web3, perception precedes valuation — and no one has proven this more consistently at scale. He has built and deployed distribution networks spanning millions of engaged community members across X, Telegram, and Discord, creating flywheel effects that traditional marketing agencies cannot replicate.

Before founding Arcadia, Mickey operated at the intersection of culture and commerce, building relationships with the tastemakers, influencers, and power brokers who define what captures market attention. His network is not transactional — it is cultivated. For Mickey, every client relationship begins with one question: what would make this impossible to ignore?

At Advising.com, Mickey leads the Special Situations Group, advising founders on launch strategy, token economics, influencer partnerships, and the go-to-market mechanics that separate projects that move markets from those that disappear into the noise.
