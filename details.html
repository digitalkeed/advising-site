About This Site

Site Details

What Is Advising.com

Advising.com is a New York–based advisory practice founded in 2024. We connect founders, executives, and teams with advisors who have already sat in the rooms that matter — boardrooms, fundraises, launches, and the quiet conversations that shape outcomes.

We are not a consultancy that bills by the hour. We are a small practice built on alignment: our advisors are compensated when you succeed, through a transparent structure that ties our interests to yours.

How It Works

Engagements begin with a conversation. You tell us what you're building, where you're stuck, and what decisions are on the table. We match you with one or more of our advisors — each chosen for relevance to your situation.

  • No retainers or hourly fees for standard advisory engagements.
  • Our 7% dilty model means we participate in success, not just activity.
  • Discretion and confidentiality are non-negotiable.
  • We work with a limited number of clients at any time to ensure focus.

Philosophy in Brief

We believe the highest-leverage decisions still happen in rooms where trust has already been built. Algorithms can inform; they cannot replace the judgment of people who have been where you're going. Our advisors have navigated Web3, venture, M&A, and the daily tradeoffs that define companies. This site exists to introduce you to them and to our way of working.

Structure & Reach

We advise on Web3 and crypto, fundraising, entrepreneurship, and deal-making. Our partners include Mickey Hardy (Special Situations Group, Web3 go-to-market) and Brian Evans (International Advisory Council, scaling and capital). For a full picture of who we are and how we think, see About Us and our Philosophy.
