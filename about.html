Conviction.

Advising.com was founded on a single, unfashionable belief: that in the age of artificial intelligence and algorithmically generated insight, the most valuable thing in the world is still a room full of the right people — and one advisor who has already sat in it.

Mickey Hardy and Brian Evans didn't build Advising.com because the market was ready for it. They built it because the market needed it. Between them, they have navigated Web3 boardrooms, venture capital pitch decks, and the quiet handshakes that close nine-figure deals. Their approach is not theoretical. It is earned.
